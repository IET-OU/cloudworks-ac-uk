Cloudworks Support

Welcome to Cloudworks

Cloudworks is being developed by the Institute of Educational Technology at The Open University. It is part of the Open University Learning Design Initiative (OULDI) project and is funded by both the Joint Information Systems Committee (JISC) and The Open University.

Support

Have a question, spotted a bug or have an issue with content on the site?

Please e-mail us at cloudworks@open.ac.uk. We may miss questions and support requests on the site itself or on other sites such as Twitter, so please e-mail us rather than hope to catch our attention elsewhere!

Your question may also be answered in our FAQ.

Feedback

Suggestions or ideas for Cloudworks?

Please add them to the Cloudworks UserVoice site or e-mail us at cloudworks@open.ac.uk

Cloudworks at-risk time

As the Cloudworks site is receiving lots of visitors at the moment, we are introducing an 'at risk' time each day from 9.30am-10am UK time. If we need to make changes to software or servers we will use do so during this time window.
