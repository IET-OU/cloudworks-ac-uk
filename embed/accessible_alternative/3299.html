OU H818 'The Networked Practitioners' Online Conference 2019

For the Open University

Conference Title

Online Communities - Dynamics of a Global Network of Standards Professionals - Shared Experiences and Common Goals?

Overview of Project

The aim of the project is to undertake an online survey of standards professionals within eight regional Closed Facebook Groups.

Many have participated in a capacity building programme to define a 3 year programme of standards development in their country.

The Closed Facebook Groups were established in order to provide remote support and sharing of best practice as each country developed their strategy documents.

The survey will assess their attitudes and interaction with the Closed Facebook Groups and will assess evidence of presence of  a community of practice or an epistemic community.
