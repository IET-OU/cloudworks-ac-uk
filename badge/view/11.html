Badge: OLDS-MOOC; 3 Weeks

For successfully completing any three weeks of the OLDS-MOOC ‘Learning Design for a 21st Century Curriculum’

Verifiers: Rebecca Galley Yishay Mor Anna Page Simon Cross Simon Kear David White Marion Manton Diana Laurilllard Gráinne Conole OLDS MOOC Simon Walker

Badge created by:

Rebecca Galley

Who has been awarded this badge?

Criteria:

  • Successful completion of any three weeks of the OLDS-MOOC ‘Learning Design for a 21st Century Curriculum’

Providing evidence:

  • When you are ready to apply for this badge click on the red button below, and insert the URL link to evidence (this might be in Cloudworks or somewhere else entirely). Please make sure that no password is required to access your evidence.
  • There are a number of ways you might provide evidence of successful completion of three week’s learning; perhaps the easiest might be to provide a link to your blog (either in Cloudworks or somewhere else) where you write posts which reflect on your learning against the weeks’ defined learning outcomes. If you do this, please make sure the relevant posts are clearly titled so they can be easily found by your verifier.

Applying for a badge

Once you have decided that you want to work towards a particular badge, make a note of the criteria and begin work.

This may mean engaging in a course or activity. The badge issuer may have been quite specific about how you could provide evidence, but if they haven't, you must decide how best to do it. Your evidence might consist of a reflective blog post, or a photo, a document, a diagram, a screen shot, or an activity stream and you will need to be able to link to all your evidence via one URL - so it needs to be held online somewhere.

If you have a number of different pieces of evidence that you want to submit, the easiest way of doing this is to set up a Cloud. Use the text box to describe your evidence and how it meets the criteria and then 'Add content', 'Add embedded content' or 'Add link' for as many pieces of evidence as you need. Then copy and paste the URL for you evidence Cloud into this page.
