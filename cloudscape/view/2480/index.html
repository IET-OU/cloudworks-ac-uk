Tuesday Synchronous Event: "Take off?" - we would like to use this session to allow you to reflect on your experience of working with OER this week and to take this forward into a wider discussion of this experience of open practices, to see if participants can answer the question "What do open practices mean for my work?".  

Wednesday activities 11. If you want to reflect on the week, write a short reflective blog post on the experience or add your reflective account of week 6 to this cloudscape.
