Learning Design Cross Institutional Network (LD-CIN)

  • 1159 views
  • 2 favourites



An initiative to establish a network and create a social place for bashing, mashing and smashing Learning Design initiatives.

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Lisette Toetenel
24 June 2015

The Learning Design team at the Open University aims to set up a Cross Institutional network to share Learning Design initiatives. We are particularly interested to hear from institutions or partners that have developed tools or processes to practically implement Learning Design.

Once the group has been set up, the agenda and aim will be formulated by its members. We envisage this group to be able to share Learning Design approaches and tools in face-to-face and online settings, as well as being able to test and validate the approaches used by other members of the group.

This Cloudscape has been set up to support this group. It is a online place for members of the Learning Design Cross Institutional Network to share ideas but will also be a space for practical matters such as Doodle polls to set up dates for meetings and a collaborative space to set the agenda. Our first meeting will take place on 07/10/15.
