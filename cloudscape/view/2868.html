Activity 9. Prepare a draft formative evaluation plan for your learning design (90 mins)

You can find a template for an evaluation plan here: http://treeves.coe.uga.edu/edit8350/EPT.html 

Please note that this plan template is probably more extensive than you’ll have time to write, but it can at least suggest some organization for your plan. Please post your plan in this Evaluation plan Cloudscape.
