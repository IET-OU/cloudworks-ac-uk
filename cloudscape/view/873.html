Welcome to gathering of OER advocates, practitioners and funders co-hosted by the Monterey Institute for Technology and Education and the William and Flora Hewlett Foundation March 2009 in Monterey, California. Sessions Clouds are organized around of the topics suggested for the meeting by current OER grantees.

Please add your thoughts and questions to the topical clouds listed here by using the Comments feature. Feel free to add text, links and media in the comment section. 

If there is an OER topic missing that you want to contribute to the group discussion, please create a new cloud and link it to this Cloudscape.
