Open Educational Resources (OER) provide a vast array of free course materials. The Open Learning Network (OLnet) is a research project, which started in Jan 2009 and is funded by the Hewlett Foundation. A major technique adopted in OLnet to uncover what has been achieved with OER is to undertake a host of facilitative workshops. This workshop explores the use/reuse of content already published in Open Educational Resource (OER) repositories. The workshop will involve an interdisciplinary audience in considering how they would use/reuse OER in their own teaching practice.
