ELESIG is an international community of researchers and practitioners from higher and further education who are involved in investigations of learners' experiences and uses of technology in learning. ELESIG members work together to share knowledge and practice and develop a shared repertoire of resources which will be of benefit to the community and the sector.

ELESIG enjoys contributory sponsorship from a number of sources including the UK Higher Education Academy.

The ELESIG community has a online social network on Ning, runs face to face and online events and awards small grants. 

So far, ELESIG has created three spaces of interest to its members on Cloudworks:

  • ELESIG - Resources to share and discuss resources produced by the ELESIG community
  • ELESIG - Readings to share and discuss publications in the field of learner experience research.
  • Contrasts and contradictions in learner experience research to share links to emerging evidence.

Please take the opportunity to join and contribute to these spaces
