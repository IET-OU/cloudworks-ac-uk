Dear Colleagues,

We are now entering the 4th of 5 discussion periods to provide UNISA colleagues with thoughts and ideas. This session is on OERs and Neil Butcher and Catherine Ngugi will be joining us.

I attach a document by Tony Mays to help start the discussion.  I think it contains some very interesting issues so we look forward to having your thoughts.

I realise that not everyuone will be able to contribute to every discussion but this topic should be of interest to those of you who have not contributed to date.

However if you do not wish to continue to be a member of this group please don't hesitate to let me know.

I have put this into a cloudscape which is a collection of clouds in the hope that I can bring all the contributions together in one place and that it will make for easier reading

I will send an  email around confirming

 

Roger
