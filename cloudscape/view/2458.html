Use phase 2 of the Ecology of Resource Design Framework to help you identify significant relationships in learners' contexts.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Watch 30s intro to phase 2 of the Ecology of Resources Design Framework.
  2. Follow the instructions in how to analyse relationships in learner context. 
  3. Create a cloud in this cloudscape called "[yourname]: eor 2 analysis]" to display your analysis (e.g. joshuau: eor2 analysis). Also add that cloud to your portfolio.
  4. Request feedback on your new cloud. Look out for other participants' clouds and provide feedback.

If you're tweeting about this week's activity, use the oldsmooc-w2 hashtag and your tweet will appear on the sidebar.

Go to OLDSMOOC-Week2-Web | OLDSMOOC-Week2-on-Cloudworks

You can leave feedback on any activity in the discussion around the corresponding cloud and/or using the feedback form.

Help: Probably the best place to ask for help with any of the activities is on the oldsmooc discussion forum, use the week and activity number for topic.
