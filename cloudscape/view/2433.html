Cloudscape created by:

Alex Addyman
29 August 2012

This cloudscape documents the development of metadata application profiles for different content types which will make up the Open University Digital Library. These will preliminarily include videos, audio files, digitised course books, archived web pages and images. Given the importance of interoperability and sharing of standards within the metadata world we feel it is important to share our developmental processes just as we have drawn invaluable resources from other digital library projects. The OUDL is currently in development so anything on this cloudscape should not be considered the finished article. This cloudscape is a way of sharing our developmental experiences with likeminded projects. For more information on the Open University Digital Library please visit the OUDL blog.
