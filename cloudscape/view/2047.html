Cloudscape created by:

ELESIG
19 March 2010

This Cloudscape has been created by ELESIG to help our members (and anyone else) keep up to date with publications in learner experience research.

The idea is that anyone can create or link to a Cloud for a paper (or article or video or presentation or book) you have read in the field of learners' experiences of e-learning.

Please feel free to add Clouds, comment on others and add to the discussions. 

We hope that this will become a space you can visit to keep up to date with this rapidly developing field. Remember you can also set your alerts to follow this CloudStream.
