This is the cloudscape for the 2010 Design Bash hosted by JISC CETIS and The LAMS Foundation.

The event will take place the day after the 2010 European LAMS Conference, on 16th July, in Oxford. The Design Bash will be held at the UOCS offices. Map and location available @ http://www.oucs.ox.ac.uk/thamessuite/

The day will start at 10.30am (tea and coffee available from 10.00am) and will finish at 4.00pm.
