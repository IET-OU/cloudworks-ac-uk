22 March 2010

OER10 will take place at the award winning Gillespie Centre at Clare College, Cambridge. The Centre, opened in 2009, accommodates up to 150 in its lecture theatre and stunning break-out rooms.

The event will consist of a stimulating and engaging mix of keynotes, project presentations and case studies, refereed papers, workshops and demos all promoting and disseminating best practice in OER.

http://www.ucel.ac.uk/oer10/index.html
