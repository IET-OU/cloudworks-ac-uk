A monthly digest of e-Learning news

Image by Jim Benton

Cloudscape created by:

Jim Ellis
26 January 2010

This digest was started by Jim Ellis in 2004 as an internal newsletter for UK Open University production staff.  It has been circulated more widely to the OU's e-Learning Community (eLC) since 2007 and now has a readership of about 750 OU staff.  The digest is issued around the middle of each month and covers news and reports on e-Learning pedagogy, technologies, practice and trends - from the public and private sectors of the UK and overseas.  It is offered here - minus some of the OU-specific items and events - for a wider web audience.

Issues 65 (Jan 2010) to 166 (Jun 2018) can be found below.  Jim left the OU in July 2018 and the Digest is now edited by Dot Coley.  Issues 167 onwards can be found on the wonderful new OU Learning Design Blog.

Note: Issues are listed using Cloudworks' default sorting order, so you may need to scroll down to find what you want!
