LMS-SECONDLIFE/OpenSim-Foreign Languages- Speech Recognition System

Oral skill language in a 3D dimension integrated in LMS-moodle

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Isabelle Martin-Fernandes
16 January 2013

My intention is to work on a project with teachers, researchers and platform developers.

My idea comes from a finding derived from observing the practices of higher education students. They experiment great difficulties in oral skills in foreign languages and more particularly in the oral expression. By using an existing LMS, I would like to integrate a tool that would allow in a first phase , training that competence deficit. I would also like to integrate a 3D world and oral practice (system with voice recognition).

Pedagogical approach: constructivism- Collaborative process.
