Pedagogical planner summit

The summit on 8th was co-organised by James Daziel, Leanne Cameron,

Sue Bennett and Peter Goodyear. The aim of the summit was to provide an opportunity

for researchers active in the area of pedagogical/activity planners, educational/learning design,

etc. to report on their recent work, share perspectives and discuss promising lines for

future work.

Learning through inquiry; teaching as design workshop

 

9th-10th Dec Invited workshop associated with Peter Goodyear's ALTC fellowship project.
