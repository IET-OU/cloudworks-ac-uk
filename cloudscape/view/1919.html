  • Content

Cloudscape created by:

Georgina Holden
4 November 2009

This cloudscape has been created to allow anyone involved in teaching and learning of design at the Open University to meet and share ideas, resources, support and guidance.  We want this to become a hub for the OU design community which is not tied to a particular course. It is not designed to replace course level discussions, but rather to complement them and add another dimension.
