This workshop will consider what can be learned from the OER Impact Study, the synthesis and evaluation of the JISC OER phase 1 and 2 projects, and OLnet, to highlight what research tells us about stakeholder engagement and the reasons for it, lessons learnt, and what the movement can do and is doing, going forward.

Presentations will be followed by an opportunity for delegates to contribute to the ongoing discussion around sustainability by considering ‘where do we go from here?’
