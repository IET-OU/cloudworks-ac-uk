Gráinne Conole

This cloudscape is associated with the Learning in an Open World Research Programme at the Open University in the UK. The space will be used to point to research projects in the programme, current activities, as well as being a more general space to discuss and aggregate relevant resources around the theme of 'openness' in an educational context and in particular issues around 'learning in an open world.
