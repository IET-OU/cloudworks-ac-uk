Cloudscape created by:

Linda Price
23 March 2010

This is a cloudscape associated with an HEA funded project that is investigating the use of evidence-based practice in using technology in learning and teaching in higher education.  To find out more about the project you can view the cloudscape at http://cloudworks.ac.uk/cloudscape/view/2045   

This cloudscape is intended as an area where practitioners can exchange ideas and experiences about using technology in teaching and learning activities with students.  We are interested in the kinds of evidence that you might use in your teaching that may ‘cause’ you to change your practices.
