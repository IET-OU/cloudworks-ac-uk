Cloudscape created by:

Gráinne Conole
25 July 2010

This Cloudscape has been set up as a space to for an 'open reflection' on the process of writing a book, currently entitled 'Designing for learning in an open world. The general reflection on the writing and development of the book will take place as a series of blog posts on www.e4innovation.com. This space will be used to aggregate these and to act as a space to invite discussion and debate on emergent issues.
