About

The 2011 Design Bash took place on Friday 30th September, at the the UOCS offices. ( http://www.oucs.ox.ac.uk/thamessuite/).

Building on the work of previous events (2010, 2009), this hands-on event was open to anyone with an interest in fostering interoperability with regard to creating, running, and sharing learning designs and provided participants with the opportunity to share and test their ideas, tools, content and designs.

 

 Agenda

  • 10.00 - 10.30  Registraton (tea/coffee)
  • 10.30 - 11.00  Round robin style introductions
  • 11.00 - 12.30  Lightening Presentations  in parallel with general discussions/"bashing"activities
  • 12.30 - 1.30  Lunch
  • 1.30 - 3.00   Continuing discussions/"bashing"activities
  • 3.00 - 4.00  Feedback session - thoughts/reflections/ways forward
