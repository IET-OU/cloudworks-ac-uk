Each group of participants in this Activity is asked to create your own Cloud (see button to the lower right 'Create cloud in this cloudscape) named 'your names Principle'.

Participant 1: Put in the link to the principle and post your introduction and question in the Cloud and set the deadline for responses.
Participant 2: Once the deadline is passed, summarise in your Cloud the points made in the discussion.

All participants: please comment on at least two other Clouds before the deadline set.
