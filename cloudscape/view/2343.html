Cloudscape created by:

Roger Mills
14 September 2011

The Cambridge International Conference on ODL has a reputation for interaction among its members.  With conference particpants limited to around 90 it is possible for all to contribute to a range of discussions.  We hope this cloudscape will help towards this objective.

The five clouds within this cloudscape are for the conference Home Groups to report their discussions such that they are available to all conference participants and others.

Home Group leaders will be the main authors but any member of the home group made add to the clouds.

In addiition there is a general cloud for the conference to which can be added comment about the conference itself as well as issues raised during the conference
