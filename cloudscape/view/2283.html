"From Innovation to Institutional Enhancement:

Operating in Challenging Times"

Date: Wednesday 15th and Thursday 16th June 2011

Venue: The Fielder Centre, University of Hertfordshire, Hatfield, Herts., UK.

Conference Theme and Format

The conference will once again have an international flavour with contributions from colleagues at the University of Calgary and the University of Queensland. It will also provide a unique opportunity to hear from a wide variety of work associated with the JISC Curriculum Design and Delivery projects, moving beyond the initial outcomes to the stories of embedding innovation.

The two days of the conference will see a mixture of presentations, discussion sessions and workshops with a Gala Dinner on the evening of the 15th June.
