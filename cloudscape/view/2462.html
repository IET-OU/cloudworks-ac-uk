What's important about context for learning design?

Image by mindmeister context & learning design

  • Content
  • Cloudstream

Cloudscape created by:

Joshua Underwood
26 November 2012

Use this cloudscape to exchange ideas about context & learning design and contextual approaches to learning design.

  • Look at your own and others' scenarios. What aspects of context do these scenarios include (or not include)? Why are these important for learning design? Brainstorm and/or mindmap your ideas on paper or otherwise.
  • Look through these thoughts on context, design and learning. Are these useful with respect to your learning design project? Why? How? 
  • Create your own mindmap for context or add your thoughts to and/or adjust this context for learning design mind map as you see fit.
  • Add your mindmaps as clouds to this cloudscape and/or discuss and post links in the discusion forum on this thread.

(Extension) Consider approaches to context and learning design.

  • Explore & share contextual approaches to learning design in more depth. Search and scan to find 2 approaches that appeal to you. Then read/skim through and summarise: how do the approaches you found describe context? how do they use context in design? See ways of thinking about context and approaches to contextual design.
