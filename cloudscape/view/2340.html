EARLI 2011 Conference

EARLI - the European Association for Research on Learning and Instruction - enables about 2000 members from 40 different countries to engage in critical dialogue. For these scholars, drawn from all parts of Europe and many parts of the world, EARLI provides a platform to contribute to current debates. Through its networks, activities and the publication of journals and book series, EARLI promotes both international and interdisciplinary cooperation.

  • http://www.earli2011.org/

The EARLI 2011 Conference will be held in the last week of August at the University of Exeter on the Streatham Campus: a campus known for being one of the most beautiful in the UK.
