Your Contributions for OU 'Learning in an Open World' conference (22-23 June 2010)

  • 3036 views

21 June 2010 - 22 June 2010
Online in Cloudworks and Elluminate

This cloudscape is a collection of multi-media contributions for the conference

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Karen Cropper
17 June 2010

This cloudscape collects together clouds with all of the multi-media contributions that have been submitted in response to the open call for the OU 'Learning in an Open World' conference (22-23 June 2010). See: http://cloudworks.ac.uk/cloudscape/view/2012

Each multimedia presentation has been embedded into a cloud so that if you have a question or a comment on any of them, you can add it to the relevant cloud rather than scrolling through a long list of unrelated messages (we had already reached 55 comments on the previous cloud).

We have been very pleased with the variety and quality of the contributions that we have received and appreciate the effort that has been put in.  If you felt any barriers to contributing yourself, we hope that this will inspire you to try out some new technology and see what you can do yourself.
