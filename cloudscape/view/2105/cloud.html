Welcome to the online debate on open educational practices!

The discussion will continue for three weeks, with each week one experts statement introducing a new topic for discussion. At the end of each week the moderator will post a summary of issues raised. There will be a Cloud for each week's discussio (see below), along with some additional Clouds for further information.
