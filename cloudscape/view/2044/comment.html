From the website:

The eighth e-learning@greenwich conference aims to:

  • Explore the dimensions of future learningscapes
  • Enable practitioners and researchers to share their use of technology in transforming the design and delivery of 21st century learning and teaching opportunities
  • Imagine the role of digital technologies and literacies in creating future learning scenarios
  • Consider the implications of future learningscapes on stakeholders
