Background and further information

This Cloudscape provides links to outputs and activities associated with the OPAL Project Work Package 3 Deliverable D3.1 'Scope of desk research & case study identification'. Deliverable D3.1 consisted of  a review of 58 case studies of Open Educational Resource Initiatives. From the case studies a set of Open Educational Practices (OEP) were identified. This report forms deliverable 'D3.1 Scope of desk research & case study identification' for the Opal project.  

Between 10th May 2010 and 9th June 2010 we are inviting the wider educational community to comment on these outputs. We are seeking views on the accuracy of the case studies and the extent to which they are representative of the current Open Educational Resource movement, discussions around the Open Educational Practice dimensions we have derived from the case studies and how these might be used to improve the quality of and innovation in the creation and use of OER. We will present a summary of the consultation at the EDEN conference in Valencia (see Session A4 on 10th June 2010)
