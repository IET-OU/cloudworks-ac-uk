The 2010 DEANZ Conference is being held from 25-28 April, 2010 at Te Papa Tongarewa, Museum of New Zealand, Wellington, New Zealand. The conference has two main themes: the challenge of building 'Quality Connections' and the'Boundless Possibilities' available to educators, through open, flexible and distance learning. The key question explored across both themes, relevant to all education sectors, is:


• How do the new places, spaces and tools of learning enhance outcomes and opportunities for learners?
 

We hope you will join us in Wellington or virtually in Cloudworks to connect with old friends, meet new colleagues and share your own experiences.
