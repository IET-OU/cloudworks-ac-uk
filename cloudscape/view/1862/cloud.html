Cloudscape created by:

3 July 2009

This Cloudscape has been set up for the 2009 European LAMS & Learning Design Conference held at The Open University 6-9th July 2009. The focus of the conference will be on Open Education and the technologies, approaches and implications of  collaborative, open acccess knowledge and resources.

 Please contribute to the discussion, and add your own clouds of relevance to the conference and its themes.
