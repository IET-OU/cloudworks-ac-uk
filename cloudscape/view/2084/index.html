WEBINAR (FACE-TO FACE DEBATE BROADCAST LIVE VIA VIDEOCONFERENCE)
Rethinking social inclusion through Web 2.0. What’s in hold for the future of language education?

  • Webinar Website
  • Help on cloudworks is here!
  • The event will take place on 28.04.2010, Turku (Finland) and will be broadcasted live via videoconferencing. The time is 11am Finnish time
    (http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/city.html?n=101 ).
  • Adobe Acrobat Connect Pro Meeting is being used, joining is very simple, go to: https://online.tse.fi/r60123567/. Attendees join the meeting as a guest (please just type in your name and enter the webinar). As attendees, you will be able to use the following functions during the discussions:
    Chat function:
    Make comments, suggestions
    Ask questions
  • Two Polls:
    Can social media and language learning lead to social equality?
    Among the recommendations given by the speakers, which one is the most
    important?
  • Invited speakers:
    • Prof. Gráinne Conole (The Open University, UK)
    • Prof. Ruth Hillman (Åbo Akademi, Finland).
    • Moderators: Prof. Fred Dervin and Prof. Eija Suomela-Salmi.
