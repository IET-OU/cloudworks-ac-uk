SCORE Event:: 1-day workshop is aimed at educators who would like to gain a broader understanding of open educational resource

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Janet Dyson
16 August 2011

The workshop will give participants the awareness, knowledge and tools necessary to navigate the path through copyright issues and Creative Commons licences with the least trouble when developing open educational resources.  In particular, it will draw upon JISC Legal’s experience in supporting projects over the past few years, and will focus on the real issues which commonly emerge. 

In keeping with JISC Legal’s usual approach, the workshop will be highly interactive, with use of voting pad questions, exercises, group discussions and case studies to ensure that the knowledge gained is practical, applicable and relevant.
