Cloudscape created by:

Amyas Phillips

"Flexible curriculum" can mean many things, including personalisation, delivery mode and responsiveness to employer needs.  This particular Cloudscape is about designing programmes and courses so that they are as easy as possible to remix, recombine, add to, subtract from, update and ghenerally maintain.  It is about the means of creating flexibility rather than its ends.
