NROC Member Meeting 2010 - OER Solutions: Enhancing Learning, Maximizing Resources

  • 1614 views

1 March 2010 - 2 March 2010
Monterey, CA

Image by NROC Network

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Dani Pedrotti
10 February 2010


From federal funding opportunities to implementation strategies, the second annual NROC Network Member Meeting will offer a chance to explore, share, and teach about creating and using OER to improve learning, while maximizing and extending your investments.

 

Participants may select workshops, interactive presentations, roundtables and hands-on sessions to share member case studies, explore professional development tools and implementation strategies and define opportunities for collaboration to maximize the impact of ARRA Stimulus Funding in creating sharable curriculum resources.

Held on the shores of California’s beautiful Monterey Peninsula, this meeting gives members a chance to share and learn from colleagues and expand their knowledge of online learning, OER in general and NROC in particular.    As we all know, virtual learning, online courses and web-based technologies are transforming education. The NROC Member Meeting gives you a chance to discover, discuss, learn and share!
