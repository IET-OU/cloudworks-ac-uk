EUROCALL 2010 Conference, Bordeaux, France

  • 1633 views

7 September 2010 - 10 September 2010
University of Bordeaux, France

Annual conference of EUROCALL on the use of computers in language learning and teaching

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Graham Davies
25 July 2010

The EUROCALL 2010 conference took place at the University of Bordeaux, France, 8-11 September 2010. The theme of EUROCALL 2010 was Networked Structures and Virtual Communities in CALL. The conference blog, with embedded CoveritLive windows, is a record of the main events, with links to the EUROCALL accounts on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and Flickr:
http://virtualeurocall2010.blogspot.com/

Videos of the plenary presentations and photos of all the events, both academic and social can be found at:
http://iufm.u-bordeaux4.fr/accueil/recherche/colloque/eurocall2010/

Check out this website for further information about EUROCALL::
http://www.eurocall-languages.org/
