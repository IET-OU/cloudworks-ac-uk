Cloudscape created by:

Gráinne Conole
1 July 2009

Discussion space for exploring how we can combine the expertise from pedagogical patterns research and learning design research to more effect design and reuse of OER?

The away day consists of researchers from the London Knowledge Lab who have been working on Pedagogical Patterns, researchers from the University of Valladolid who have also being doing patterns research, collaborative scripts and learning design and researchers from the Open University who have been working on OERs and Learning Design. 

We are trying to attempt to combine these research areas and to come up with a collective set of research questions and tools/methods to help facilitate the design and reuse of OER.

This follows on from an initial workshop attempting to put these things together, the cloudscape from the outputs from the workshop is here. 

Feel free to join in the combination.
