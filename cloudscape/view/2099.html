Welcome!... to the online workspace before/during/after ODET 2010: Online Deliberation Emerging Tools (Leeds, 30 June), a workshop co-located with the Fourth International Conference on Online Deliberation (30 June–2 July, 2010).

Participants are asked to sign up, and then "Create Cloud in this Cloudscape" — a space you own, to provide info/slides/movies about your approach, and the challenges of seeing it go to the next level. Your cloud will then be listed below...
