Learning Design as a term originated in the technical community and began to gain prominence around 2004, following the development of the educational mark-up language at the Open University of the Netherlands. Since then it has been appropriated by others in a much broader sense - encompassing 'Designing for Learning'. This cloudscape is intended as a space for thoseinterested in learning design research and its application to share and discuss relevant clouds.
