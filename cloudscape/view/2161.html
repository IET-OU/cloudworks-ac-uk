Cloudscape created by:

K802 Module Team
5 August 2010

K802 Critical practice with children and young people is a module in The Open University’s MA in Childhood and Youth. This Cloudscape has been designed as a space where students of the module’s 2010 presentation can share, and search for, resources to support their work on the module.

The individual Clouds relate to particular categories of work with children and young people (such as professional sectors, age groups, national jurisdictions). Full instructions on using and contributing to the Cloudscape can be found on the K802 website.
