Online, remote Elluminate session delivered by Viewpoints, the University of Ulster's JISC-funded curriculum design project

  • Content

Cloudscape created by:

Karen Virapen
8 June 2010

This Elluminate session will give a brief overview of Viewpoints, the University of Ulster's JISC-funded curriculum design project, our key objectives, and the tools that we aim to produce.  Participants will be able to view some sample workshop materials and a walkthrough of our planned online tool functionality.

Two interactive activities during the session will allow participants to try out the workshop approach for themselves. We will also share our user experiences to date by demonstrating a range of 'user stories' in video, audio, photo and quote form. The session will also cover the work done to embed Viewpoints tools within our institution, and end with a discussion of future directions and a structured question and answer session.

To register for this online session, go to http://bit.ly/Gm9Ni.

A recording of this session will also be available after the event, if you sign up and are subsequently unable to attend.

Please distribute to anyone you feel would be interested.

You can Tweet during the session and mark your tweets with the hashtag #jiscviewpoints if you want to be included in our Twitter stream.
