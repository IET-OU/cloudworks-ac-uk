The learner experience of transition

  • 1349 views

14 July 2010 - 15 July 2010
METROPOLITAN HOTEL, LEEDS and UNIVERSITY OF LEEDS

An opportunity to critically examine the nature of the learner experience in relation to transition

  • Content
  • Cloudstream
  • Clouds

Cloudscape created by:

Malcolm Ryan
2 June 2010

Our third Summer Symposium provides an opportunity for the ELESIG community to come together at a residential event to critically examine the nature of the learner experience in relation to transition and contribute to future SIG planning and developments. The programme for the event will include an opportunity to engage with members who have managed ELESIG Small Grants Projects, consider what we have learnt as a community since our inception, plan for our continued future and participate in a number of keynotes and parallel workshops and sessions on the theme of ‘transition’ sponsored by Aim Higher.

The symposium is free to ELESIG members. Places for the fully sponsored residential element of the symposium will be limited to 30, but we will also accept applications for day delegates.

Details and booking on our Ning site.
