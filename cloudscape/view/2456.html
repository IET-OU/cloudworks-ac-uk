Cloudscape created by:

Yishay Mor
21 November 2012

You can complete the OLDS MOOC on your own, but we believe your experience will be much more fruitful if you have some friends for the journey. We strongly encouge you to form a study circle.This could be based on:

 

  • Your project team, and 2-3 other teams with similar projects.
  • A group of participants in your institution
  • Participants you identify as having similar interests or living nearby. 

 

You can use services such as http://www.meetup.com/ to set up your local study circle.

Set up a "shared space" for your study circle. Use a cloudscape, a facebook group, a google group, a wiki or blog, or any other social technology you are comfortable with.

Create a cloud for your study circle - noting its themes or charactaristics (if any), and linking to its shared space. Add that cloud to this cloudscape.

Please use the feedback cloud to share any insights, comments or questions about this activity.
