Cloudscape created by:

ELESIG
11 April 2010

This Cloudscape has been created by ELESIG to disseminate the resources of various kinds that our members have created in the area of learner experience research. Here we hope to share the resources we have created with the wider sector. Comments and suggestions are welcome from all.

ELESIG will create or link to a Cloud for a resource - which may be a paper, article, report, guidelines, write up of an event, discussion summary, webinar recordings, or any other resource created by the ELESIG community and its members.

Please feel free to comment and add to the discussions.   If you edit a cloud you might want to check out our

Guide to tagging for ELESIG

and

Using Cloudworks: a Guide for ELESIG Members

The clouds are categorised as

  • Approaches to learner experience research
  • Methods in learner experience research
  • Findings of learner experience research
  • Impact of  learner experience research
