Cloudscape created by:

Rebecca Galley
10 May 2010

Recently LSBU made the decision to move to a 20-credit module framework. The aim of this workshop is to provide course teams with an opportunity to design their new modules using a series of empirically tested learning design tools from a range of projects including the Open University Learning Design Initiative (OULDI) of which the LSBU is a partner.

The structure of the workshop takes its lead from the 4 strands:

  • Assessment
  • Employability
  • Use of Technology
  • Inclusive curriculum

Each of these strands will act as a 'lens' through which to examine new modules.

We hope that this Cloudscape will continue to evolve after the workshop and will be of interest to both those in and outside of the university. Please feel free to add to it!
