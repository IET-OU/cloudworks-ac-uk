This Cloudscape provides links to the Clouds and Cloudscapes used in week 2 of the OLDS MOOC. Week 2 explores approaches for inquiring into, describing, and using context in learning design.

Use this Cloudscape to navigate week 2 activities within Cloudworks.

Alternatively navigate activities directly from OLDSMOOC Week 2 web page.

Activities:

  • Plan your activity for the week
  • Start to contextualise your design challenge (using scenarios)
  • What's important about context for learning design?
  • Consider participants & forces (using personas & force maps)
  • Consider resources (using EoR phase 1, phase 2, phase 3)
  • Review & Share
  • Discuss & Reflect
  • Curate & Evaluate

You can leave feedback on any activity in the discussion around the corresponding cloud and/or using the feedback form.

Help: Probably the best place to ask for help with any of the activities is on the oldsmooc discussion forum, use the week and activity number for topic.
