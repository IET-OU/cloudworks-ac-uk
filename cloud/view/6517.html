<H2>1.0 Welcome to Succeed with Math!</H2>
<P>Why study math? There are at least three reasons:</P>
<OL>
<LI>There is a lot of evidence that people who are numerate — that is, people
 who aren’t afraid of figures and are happy discussing data — are much more
 likely to advance in their careers and earn more than those who are intimidated
 by the very thought of working with numbers.</LI>
<LI>Math is a lot of fun — you’ll find puzzles and fun stuff in <I>Succeed with
Math</I> that you can use to impress your friends and family. </LI>
<LI>And, most important, math is a fundamental and beautiful subject, a key to
 understanding the universe and ourselves. </LI></OL>
