Downlights are the most essential form of lighting, which has the potentiality to increase the look and feel of the interior of the house. The trend of utilizing Downlights is increasing at a rapid pace because of the latest features associated with it. Downlights are also called as spot lights, pot lights and also can lights which can be easily installed into the hollow during construction time.

You can use fire rated downlights to prevent the spread of the fire. It is designed as a safety measure. The Downlights is mainly used to maintain the fire integrity of the ceilings. Bear in mind that not all the Downlights are fire rated but still, it’s quite important to know when and where the Downlights should be used.

With the installation of recessed Downlights, a hole must be made in the ceiling in order to mount the downlights. The presence of this hole decreases the fire integrity of the building ceiling.
