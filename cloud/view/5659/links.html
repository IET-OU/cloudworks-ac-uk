Curriculum business models

Cloud created by:

Graham Healing
15 August 2011

Where: Library Seminar Rooms 1 & 2, Walton Hall

Time: 10.00 - 12.30, 20 September 2011

This event was recorded and is available to OU staff via Stadium. 

'Curriculum business models - reshaping the design process': Martin Weller

Martin Weller led an exploration of some of the work he has been involved in over the past year, exploring what it offers and how it might work, and invited discussion of issues arising.

'Where is the use in reuse?': Chris Pegler

Is it really possible to create a synposis of a 90k word thesis as a set of 36 cards?

Chris 'reused' the high points of her recently submitted dissertation on reuse of digital online learning resources through the medium of her reusable card game on reuse. Yes, reuse does generate those chicken-egg questions of what comes first - sharing or use and an emphasis on practice needs to be drawn on to make sense of the promise of reuse, including reuse of open educational resources (OER). Yes this will pose serious questions and share interesting insights.

Open Resources and Reuse - Launch of new Special Interest Group: Chris Pegler

Chris will launch a new eLearning Community Special Interest Group (SIG) on the topic of Open Resources and Reuse. If you are interested in reuse and the impact/influence of open educational resources then come along to this launch meeting. This is a SIG which will recruit members outside as well as within the OU to focus on the reuse impact of OER, one of the topics of the new OLNET-based project ORIOLE.
