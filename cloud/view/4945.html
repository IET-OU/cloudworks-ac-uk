The University of Hertfordshire’s Learning and Teaching Institute, the UK Higher Education Academy and JISC infoNet are delighted to announce their joint event:

Sixth International Blended Learning Conference
“From Innovation to Institutional Enhancement – operating in challenging times”

Wednesday 15th – Thursday 16th June 2011
The Fielder Centre, University of Hertfordshire, Hatfield, Hertfordshire, UK

The past decade has seen great innovation in blended learning, both in the UK and internationally. The knowledge base and experience in creating and supporting technology enhanced learning is significant. In order for this to impact the student experience in a lasting and meaningful way the innovative practice needs to translate into enhancement. The Sixth International Blended Learning Conference aims to explore this process for institutions whilst they operate in such challenging times.
