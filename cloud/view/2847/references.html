'Living political ideas' (DD306) is an Open University course with a highly innovative approach to delivering the course materials. 

Students are able to access course materials that integrate video, audio, teaching texts, interactive media and study guidance into an unprecedented digital learning journey.

By combining audio-visual materials with primary theory texts and interactive software tools the course team, chaired by Dr Jef Huysmans and Dr Raia Prokhovnik, succeeded in making political theory alive for students.

The British Universities Film and Video Council recognised the Politics and International Studies Department of the OU being at the cutting edge of innovations in teaching by giving the course the Award for Courseware and Curriculum 2009.
