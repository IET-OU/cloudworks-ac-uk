How to add captions to a (YouTube) Video

I'm collating available resources, guidance and experiences about making embeddable videos more accessible. Unfortunately Vimeo has decided not to support video captioning (see this thread for interest http://vimeo.com/forums/topic:4072 ) but YouTube has.

If you know about any resources, or you've got feedback on any of them please join in :-)
