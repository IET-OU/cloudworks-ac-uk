Penny Bentley's #moocskills Prototype Kicks Off

  • Favourite
  • 839 views
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xjIGo5hM0UMPkiQhlDP7f41iz6eElwOCRbnzYlZhKXQ/edit?usp=sharing

Cloud created by:

Penny Bentley
9 February 2013

My project is a course designed to bring academocs/teachers up to speed with working online for teaching and moocing.
At this stage I've mapped things out and have most of the e-frastructure in place such as @moocskills, #moocskills,  moocskills.com (still needs domain mapping prob. sorted), moocskills@gmail.com ets.
I have 2 volunteer guineapigs who I contacted thismorning. 
My prototype begins with finding real people "out there" who best represent the learners for my course. 
I'll begin by understanding their "personas" so I can personalise the course to their needs.  

My Google Drive housed at moocskills@gmail.com will be one of my prototype tools. It's a pity that a public Google Doc can't be embedded in a cloud...maybe I'm overlooking something. 

I'd like some more guineapigs, so please add to my Google Doc to join the class.
