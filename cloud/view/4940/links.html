Start the year in Las Vegas. This event shows the trends in devices. Apple will not be there but, as reported by AP,  DisplaySearch analyst Richard Semenza has estimated that a hundred different tablet models are in development. Some of these will be used for e-books or forms of publishing. 

TV sets will also feature.

"Basically, the TV will look like your smart phone and have access to the Internet," said Gary Shapiro, president and CEO of the Consumer Electronics Association, which organizes the show.
