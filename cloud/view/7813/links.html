Mini survey re DYI Multimedia

  • Favourite
  • 710 views

Cloud created by:

Alice （Xin） Huang
25 January 2013

I have been luring around in the DIY multimedia project team, and I had an interesting discussion with my colleagues. As Educational designers, we are among those people who try to download and install free software at work to DIY multimedia element, one issue we are having is learning how to use these software. Often there is not budget for us to attend external workshop. So we have search online and find free tutorial to learning by ourselves. It works for us although it would be much better if there is an experienced designer taking us through some basics as a tutor and bringing us to working knowledge level. Compare to commercial training company, I think the cost for getting an experience user to tutor us would much cheaper. So if this is the case, would you pay the tuition by yourself to upskill your DIY multimedia capacity? 

I have created two poll activities below, please click on the link to vote:

1.  Would you be happy to pay by yourself to learn software so you can DYI multimedia?

2. Which software(s) do you want to learn to be able to DIY multimedia?
