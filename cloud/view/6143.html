IET TCM Event: What I learnt from 'Flickr Photo a Day'

  • Favourite
  • 1093 views

3 April 2012

Location: Jennie Lee Building Meeting Room 1 Ground Floor

Cloud created by:

Lynda Davies
29 February 2012

Abstract: Using services such as Flickr and Blipfoto, a practice has been established of taking, and sharing, a photo a day, over the period of a year. In this talk Martin and Juliette will talk about their experience of doing this, what they learnt and the implications for education.

Presenter: Professor Martin Weller

Location: Jennie Lee Building Meeting Room 1 Ground Floor

It was an excellent session and useful to have some creative thinking time about how we might use images and the image bank itself (Flickr and blip) to support exploring topics and themes.

I found both presenters very engaging and I am sure we were all impressed at their dogged determination to work through the challenge of taking one picture every day come what may!

My colleagues and I in the library are thinking about  how we might use the approach to help with staff development and even possibly a recruitment task!

I might even sign up for the challenge myself after feeling inspired by the session.

Thanks!
