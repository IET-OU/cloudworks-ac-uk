Chris Tyler, Accessibility Solutions Team, Department for Work and Pensions

The objectives of this workshop are to:

  • Provide a business-led view of why accessibility is important to business;
  • Identify why different businesses have stood up to deliver accessible solutions; 
  • Showcase the real practical examples of what can be done and the benefits (referencing case studies within the business case developed by OneVoice for Accessible ICT Coalition); 
  • Highlight methods/resources to help other businesses get started/improve.
