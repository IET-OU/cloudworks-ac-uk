Gerry Hanley Using OER today: empowering your teaching and your students’ learning with MERLOT

 

 

Education is one of the most powerful social and economic change agents in the world. How do we help teachers and learners to make sense of this? 

Open doors: OER, OpenCourseWare (OCW), Open textbooks, Open access journals, Open = free of costs, freedom to use, free to use in your own language. 

The web provides access to many open resources but do the doors open up powerful educational resources and services?

 

Learning by doing: what will you manipulate, what will you measure and how will you organiise data, key questions around an OER on thermodynamic equilibrium.

 

Important to start from the learning outcomes - what do you want your students to be able to do?

 

MERLOT provides you with tools and resources that enable you to design the learning that’s right for your students. 

 

www.merlot.org
