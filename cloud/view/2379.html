yes I think its a good use of the site, i have been doing that more and more recently. I am finding it has replaced my use of delicious. Also is good because everyone can enrich entries - add links or references and of course have a conversation around the cloud. Not quite clear what you are asking? You can have a dynamic twitter stream appear for a cloudscape (ie a collection of clouds), when you set up the cloudscape simply put in the details of the twitter tag you want. You can't at the moment have a twitter stream for a cloud. We have also been using twatterkeeper to archive tweet streams. A good example to look at is the alt-c cloudscape http://cloudworks.ac.uk/index.php/cloudscape/view/1870 which includes the twitter stream right at the bottom but also has a link to the twatter archive. Hope this helps! Give me a shout if not!
