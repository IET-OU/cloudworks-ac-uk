Comment 2 by Margery Clarke

Margery Clarke
2:36pm 17 November 2009 Permalink


Moodlers say that, in terms of designing online forums, academics are worried about

  1. the amount of time this may take
  2. How students' contributions can be moderated
  3. Will students use their own spaces anyway through facebook?

Would students like to have a 'Facebook' type presence within Moodle which academics and students could both contribute to?

But Facebook isn't  good for dissemination - only  for social networking.  Cloudworks could be academic discourse between students and academics.

Cloudworks could work, for example, to help find others' experience of moderating student forums in Moodle.
