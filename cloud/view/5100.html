Abstract: Juliette Culver made the Cloudworks software that powers Cloudworks avalable as the open-source CloudEngine project in October 2010.  Nine months on we will be exploring:

  1. What CloudEngine is, what it has to offer
  2. The process, the journey, the people
  3. Why we went open-source, what we're learning
  4. Our roadmap
  5. How you can get involved

Find out more about CloudEngine at, http://getcloudengine.org/

Presenter:  Dr Nick Freear (IET)

Location:  Jennie Lee Building Meeting Room 1 Ground Floor
