Web Collage:

Likes:

  • Ability to work with other platforms such as Moodle and in this case add functionality that current versions of Moodle claim but currently struggls with such as adding groups (nice from imported lists of names)
  • Opportunity to visually represent info which is of value and can be helpful compared to large text blocks
  • Still maturing as a concept and is likely therefore to take into accont emergent technologies

Challenges:

  • As a caveat to first like - would need to be sure there was housekeeping completed on student list as they don't tend to self remove from completed modules.
  • Time consuming to make visual repressentations and these could be confusing / complex if not considered carefully
  • Doesn't account for all of the parameters of a session such as time and as it is not mature, it doesn't feel like it fully addresses the job
  • Limited engagement so far with discussion (lack of constructive comments) means this aspect adds little at this time

4T's model:

Likes:

  • Really clear sections and visual representation is slick and functional
  • Technology section serves as a reminder to consider utilisation of computer based and other tech
  • Meets the aims of KISS and therefore could be applied in different levels of sophistication by a range of practitioners
  • Would make a useful addition / could replace standard SP - sugest could become 5T's to incorporate 'Themes' such as English and maths, PREVENT, E&D etc.

Challenges:

  • Despite claims to follow CSCL it doesn't inherently facilitate use of computers or other technology
  • Would need to go through a 'process' to get the nice neat swim lanes and this might not be so simple as the end representation implies
