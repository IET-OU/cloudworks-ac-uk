If you wish to break the shackles of stressful routines, and escape into the lap of nature, then it's time to visit Lonavala. The hill station is packed with some of the most romantic and picturesque places that make Lonavala a must-see hill station. If you have planned to visit the hill station this summer, make your bookings online in Fariyas Lonavala -one of the posh five star hotels in Lonavala.

 Experience a World-Class Sojourn in Fariyas Lonavala

Fariyas Lonavala is one of the most opulent five-star hotels in Lonavala, making it one of the most sought-after hotels in the place. The hotel stacks its patrons with a plethora of world-class amenities and best-in-class comforts. Despite offering world-class amenities, the hotel also offers a plethora of entertainment and recreational options to patrons. Recreational facilities in the resort include swimming pools, beauty salons, spa centers, fitness clubs, and so on.

Wine and dine options in the resort include a tranquil fine dine restaurant and an exuberant all day dining restaurant. The fine dine restaurant- ‘Fusion’ is one of a kind that offers culinary delights from both the Indian and Oriental cuisines. The all day dining restaurant- ‘Coffee Shop’ caters to 'desi' food lovers by offering exotic culinary delights and offerings from the Indian cuisine. The signature bar in the resort called ‘Tavern’ houses some of the most striking signature drinks and spirits.
