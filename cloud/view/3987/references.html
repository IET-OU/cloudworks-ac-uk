Comments from Barry:

"PIVOT is the Professional Identity and Values Organisation Tools and offers videos and downloadable exercises for users to have a go at a set of techniques for self-directed enquiry and self-managed learning.

A lot of work went into their creation and this conference seems like a good opportunity to make them more widely available.

Here is a link to the PIVOT pages: www.open.ac.uk/pbpl/projects/pivot"
