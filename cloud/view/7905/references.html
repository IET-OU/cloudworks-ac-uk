Persona Card - Learning Design

Name: Isabel

Gender: Female

Age: 35

Lives in a city in Portugal with her family likes a lot of what she do, she is a teacher of secondary school.

Education and experience

She has a degree, is a teacher for 10 years in a secondary school and a vocational course coordinator.

Role and responsibilities

She teaches Area Integration and ICT, also performs coordination of one of the professional courses, motivating trainers to conduct practical training to enable students to professional requirements.

Technical skills

Master the use of computers and software. His interest lies with image editing software.

Subject domain skills and knowledge

ICT, image editing software, interaction with others, video

Motivation and desires

It is a person who always liked technology and tries to be as updated as possible, but do not not know how to use the software you have the discipline that teaches.

Goals and expectations

Being able to integrate new tools in their pedagogical activity, capturing the attention of students and motivating them to go further.

Obstacles to their success

The internet connection is not always the best, being slow and creating some obstacles in conducting online activities. Also do not always have the computer room available, not all students possuiem computer.

Unique assets

However all rooms are equipped with projector and wireless network.
