Day 2: Tuesday, November 15 (The upcoming EEE project: prospection)

09:30-11:00: Session 21: General presentation and discussion of the EEE project (Coordinator: Carlos Delgado)

  • General EEE presentation - motivation and objectives (30 min): Carlos Delgado
  • The UC3M EEE view (10 min): Carlos Delgado
  • The UPF EEE view (10 min): Davinia Hernández-Leo
  • The UVA EEE view (10 min): Yannis Dimitriadis
  • Short discussion

11:00-11:30: Coffee break

11:30-12:00 Reporting back from Learn3 and Sofocles sessions

12:00-14:00: Session 22: Providing innovative and significant scenarios  (Coordinator: Davinia Hernández-Leo)

  • Group activities based on three EEE scenarios, as related to the main issues to be tackled during the project (60 min.)
    • Experimental-based learning with emphasis in K-12 education and direct haptic interaction
    • Exploration-based learning with emphasis in secondary education and open spaces beyond classroom
    • Project-based learning with emphasis in engineering education, laboratories and artifact design
  • Plenary session with rapporteurs from the groups and generation of a global concept maps of issues (60 min)

14:00-15:30: Lunch

15:30-17:30: Session 23: Issues and solutions related to the scenarios of the EEE project (Coordinator: Yannis Dimitriadis)

  • Group and plenary activities on solutions regarding the main EEE issues. Connection to the scenarios produced in the previous session.

17:30-17:45: Coffee break

17:45-18:45: Session 24: Synthesis of discussion regarding EEE  (Coordinator: Davinia Hernández-Leo)

21:15-23:00: Dinner (city center)
