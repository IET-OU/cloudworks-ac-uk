ELESIG
11 April 2010

ELESIG is a community of practice for researchers and practitioners interested in the learner experience of elearning.

ELESIG is an international community of researchers and practitioners in education and training who are involved in investigations of learners' experiences and uses of technology in learning. ELESIG members work together to share knowledge and practice and develop a shared repertoire of resources which will be of benefit to the community and the sector.

ELESIG is based in a Ning social network

Membership is free and while many members are from HE and FE, we also welcome anyone interested in the learner experience of learning technologies e.g., from HE, FE, schools, workplace and lifelong learning, commercial training or any other sector.

ELESIG runs three face to face symposia per year and a number of online events as well as discussions and opportunities to network online. It is run by a Management Team of volunteers from various educational institutions and a Community Manager.

ELESIG has created two spaces of interest to its members on Cloudworks:

  • ELESIG - Resources to share and discuss resources produced by the ELESIG community
  • ELESIG - Readings to share and discuss publications in the field of learner experience research.
