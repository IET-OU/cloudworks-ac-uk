Extra content

Those in attendance at the User Generated Content Stall will be

  • Martin Weller, Professor of Educational Technology in the Institute of Educational Technology
  • Louise Olney, Project Manager of the User Generated Content strand of the VLE Roadmap Acceleration Project
  • Simone Davies, Project Manager of the Personalisation strand of the Roadmap Acceleration Project
  • Kim Green, Production Manager, FELS, who will be advising on her experience of Open Design Studio from the student perspective

We will be presenting the latest designs for user generated content tools, including the new OU annotation tool, notes for module study planners and the new OU personal start page for students (iGoogle) - a fully customisable space for users to populate as they choose with OU and other Google gadgets.

Come and see us at the Learn About Fair, where hopefully we can answer your questions about developments on the User Generated Content and Personalisation Strands of the Roadmap Acceleration project, and you will be able to watch Martin Weller's collection of video clips and discuss with him issues they raise relating to user generated content and education. Kim Green from FELS will also be on hand to provide the student perspective and show some examples of her work and that of fellow students using Open Design Studio.

Simone Davies
