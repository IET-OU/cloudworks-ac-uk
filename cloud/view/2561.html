Announcement 

Next week Monday 26th our second Hot Seat will start in our Networked Learning Community. This time we will have George Siemens and Stephen Downes hosting a discussion on the Impact of Learning on Networked Technologies. 

This discussion will be held for a week in the Hot Seat forum of our networked learning community but as a special event George and Stephen will moderate a synchronous teleconference debate to introduce their topic using Elluminate. 

This teleconference will be held on Monday 26th at 12:15 Atlantic time. Please consult this link to check out what time this will be for you! 

http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?month=10&day=26&year=2009&hour=12&min=15&sec=0&p1=286

 To join this discussion log in using this link to get access to the Elluminate room:

https://sas.elluminate.com/m.jnlp?sid=2008104&password=M.0A68F27C6846C5A75D6F94199C2118

 Try to log in a few minutes early as you might need to install a Java plugin. 

The Hot Seat discussion during the entire week will be in our forum:

http://www.networkedlearningconference.org.uk/index.php/forum

 See you next week,
