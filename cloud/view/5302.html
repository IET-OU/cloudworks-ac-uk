Isabella, I note that you are able to enlarge the embedded text by clicking on the full screen icon on the right-hand side at the bottom of the screen containing the script.  With regards to getting at a version of the text that you could print, you can achieve this by clicking on the “share” icon on the far left-hand side, at the bottom of the screen with the text and then send the script to your email address.  Once the script has been directed to your email “In” box you can “View” the text and then choose “Save as” and direct the text to a Rich text formatted document.  The disadvantage of this method is that in the transfer the formatting of the original text is lost and all diagrams become simply a series of words.  For this particular bit of information you can retrieve a PDF file from the internet: http://www.lancs.ac.uk/fss/organisations/netlc/past/nlc2010/abstracts/PDFs/Conole_2.pdf .  I hope that this helps.  Genie
