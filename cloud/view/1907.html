Learning Activity Management System

Cloud created by:

Sheila MacNeill
7 July 2009

LAMS is a tool for designing, managing and delivering online collaborative learning activities. It has a visual authoring environment for creating sequences of learning activities. These activities can include a range of individual tasks, small group work and whole class activities based on both content and collaboration.

To get a demo account for LAMS, sign up at

http://demo.lamscommunity.org/

then you can log in at

http://demo.lamscommunity.org/lams/

For user documentation for LAMS, see

http://wiki.lamsfoundation.org/display/lamsdocs/Home

- see especially the animated walkthroughs at

http://wiki.lamsfoundation.org/display/lamsdocs/LAMS+Tutorials

Finally, the LAMS Community has lots of LAMS Learning Designs you can download - the community is at http://www.lamscommunity.org

 - and to see how to download a LAMS sequence from the Community then import it into the LAMS Author area of your LAMS account (eg, get one from the demo site noted above) - see this animation:

http://wiki.lamsfoundation.org/display/lamsdocs/Importing+and+Exporting+Sequences
