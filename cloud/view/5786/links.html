Welcome to the NLC2012 Hot Seat Series

 

Prior to the Networked Learning 2012 conference on April 2nd, 3rd and 4th in Maastricht, The Netherlands we are offering an exciting series of online hot seats hosted here by some of the leading thinkers in the field. From October 2011 we will have a Hot Seat each month till the conference starts.

These Hot Seats are free for all to join and all discussions will be public.

Please contact Maarten de Laat (maarten.delaat(ad)ou.nl) for further information.

It would be nice if you could add your location on the Member map, so we get a feel of where people are from.

 

This hot seat will be hosted by Peter Goodyear from October 10 - 14 on The architecture of productive learning networks
