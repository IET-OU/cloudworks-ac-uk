Design Principles for Effective website design

  • Favourite
  • 214 views

Cloud created by:

Carol Anissa
18 July 2017

They say beauty is in the hands of the beholder, in the same way, an effective website design is judged by the users of the website. There are any factors that affect the website usability but we will focus on few that can be your starting point.

1)   Purpose

A good website always caters to the needs of the users. Think, are your website visitors are looking for entertainment, some interaction, some service, or buying something. Whatever their cause be if you do not fulfill their cause you need to rethink of your website design.

2)   Communication

People on the web tends to get some information. People visit the website to get gain knowledge about particular topics. It is important to know that the way you communicate with your website will be the starting point for others to judge you. By this, all your website users can be converted into customers.

3)   Fonts

You might have heard of this one, or not. But fonts do help people to take decisions. You need to use fonts that will impact the buying decisions of the users. Yes, I’m not kidding .My friend from Web Design Agency in Auckland just changed his website fonts and his website sales boosted exponentially. You can also experiment with some fonts and see how the results come out.

4)   Colors

Study Psychology of colors and use them on your website. If you are really willing to get some orders, use colors that influence the behavior of your users. Colors do affect when it comes to buying decision. It is easier to get customers to trust by using light colors than using dark colors which can create a sense of danger in them.

5)    Images

No doubt one of the most influential elements when it comes to website design. Human images can influence users to buy more. If you use the images of other human people this will create some connection between the users and the people in the photo. Users might be able to relate their lives to the people in the photo. Use a lot of images that can influence the behavior of the users.

6)   Navigation

What type of navigation will you be using? The best is to use easy to navigate website. No need rocket science for the website. The users now days prefer simple websites. If your website is simple it is good enough to get sales. But if it is not, it will not get that much of sales. If the users need to think on your website, this is a down side of the website. No need to keep things complex. Use simple designs that are easy to understand.

7)   Mobile friendliness

Mobile friendliness is one thing that can make or break your website. If you really want to design an effective website, you need to think mobile-only websites. These websites are in demand and users are now use to mobile friendly websites. More than 80% of the users are now buying stuff right from their mobile. Either design an app for your product or make the design worth browsing on your website. Most of the small business owners don’t realize importance of mobile only website until it is too late. You need to understand that it is a basic necessity and people will have to go for mobile friendly website in the end.

To conclude it all. You need to design websites for the users. If your user cannot use the website, you cannot force them to. It is your responsibility to understand the user and design websites that are for them.
