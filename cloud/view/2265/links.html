Ascilite 2009 conference, Auckland Dec 2009

Cloud created by:

The Cloudworks Team
11 September 2009

December 2009 sees the 26th annual Ascilite conference.

The Acilite conferences focus on the use of technology in education. There is usually a nice balance of pragmatic case studies and examples, grounded in the literature. Excellent conference with good papers and a great social atmosphere. Auckland is a pretty fantastic location too!
