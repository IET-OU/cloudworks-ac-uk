ELESIG
11 April 2010

If you create a cloud that is relevant to ELESIG and would like to add it to the ELESIG Cloudscapes, such as

ELESIG - Resources (for resources created within the ELESIG community or by its members)

or

ELESIG - Readings (for papers, articles, videos, etc. from the literature or the web that you wish to bring to the attention of ELESIG members)

 then please tag the cloud as ELESIG

and Add to Cloudscape.

Other tags you may wish to consider are listed below:

  • Name of the research methodology
  • Name of institution, e.g., University of Bradford
  • Location, e.g., Oxford
  • Country, e.g., Australia
  • Name of project or initiative, e.g., SOLSTICE
  • Name of VLE or other product, e.g., Moodle, Blackboard, Wimba, PebblePad, DimDim, Elluminate
  • Job Titles
  • Subject / discipline
  • Level, e.g., Graduate Student
