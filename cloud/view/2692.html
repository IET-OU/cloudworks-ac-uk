Keynote: Helen Beetham and Rhona Sharpe at the JISC online conference

This cloud has been set up to live blog, add resourses and further discussion, referring to Helen Beetham and Rhona Sharpe's talk "Responding to learners", scheduled in the programme of the fourth JISC online conference.

In this keynote the authors want to debunk two myths about learners and technology - 'digital natives' and 'learners expectations' - and replace them with a more nuanced understanding of what learners want from educators, and how we might respond.

  • Delegates will also be interested in a resource pack, also called Responding to Learners, which has been produced to synthesise the outcomes from the Learner Experiences of e-Learning theme of the JISC e-Learning Programme. The programme funded a total of ten projects from 2005 to 2009, and had the sustained involvement of over 200 learners and more than 3000 survey respondents to explore learners’ perceptions of and participation in technology-enhanced learning in a digital age.
