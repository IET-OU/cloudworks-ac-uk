ABSTRACT

The "Simulation" began as a technique of teaching Middle East Politics through the internet. In essence, it is an extended role-playing game conducted by e-mail and web site whereby students, divided into teams of two or three playing a Middle Eastern role, will respond to a likely scenario in order to further their interests. A fuller description is at : 

http://www.learningdesigns.uow.edu.au/exemplars/info/LD3/index.html
