Simon described Tools that can help Social Spaces or Social Learning work:

  • Cloudworks
  • Flashmeeting/Skype
  • Compendium - as Excel for ideas
  • Screen recording and annotation (linked to Compendium)
  • Cohere - as way to annotate and share ideas (if you have cohere plug-in comments appear in sidebar for this page)
  • ELLIment - online tool for mentoring using the ELLI framework (search cloudworks for more on ELLI).
  • SocialLearn -current focus on PhD and researchers in OU to build space to join diverse resources
  • OpenSocial as glue - example is iGoogle gadget that acts as people recommender from cloudworks information.
