Demoing embeds, part 4 - Google Docs

  • Favourite
  • 984 views

Cloud created by:

Nick Freear
31 August 2011

Google Docs spreadsheets and forms can be embedded in Cloudworks and CloudEngine powered sites. Google Docs documents, presentations and other items cannot be embedded yet.

Note this is BETA functionality. Error reports to cloudworks@open.ac.uk.

Here's how:

  1. Publish the spreadsheet or form - "Share | Publish to the Web..." in the Google Docs interface;
  2. Copy the URL (link) from the Google Docs dialog box, or from your browser address bar. Accepted examples are below.
  3. Go to Cloudworks, log in and visit one of your clouds;
  4. Press the "Add embedded content" button. Paste your link in the URL field.
  5. Remove anything after the "#" (if present), and optionally add a height in pixels, eg. http://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=VERY-LONG-KEY#height=1200
  6. Press the "Add embedded content" button. Go back and edit the height as necessary.

Examples of the accepted URL formats:

  • http://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=reBYenfrJHIRd4voZfiSmuw
  • https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/pub?key=0AonYZs4MzlZbcmVCWWVuZnJKSElSZDR2b1pmaVNtdXc#gid=0
  • https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?hl=en_GB&formkey=dFJtUEJTQlZiVEs5R3B5ZFpRd3ZRMFE6MA#gid=0
  • https://doc.google.com/spreadsheet/embeddedform?formkey=dFJtUEJTQlZiVEs5R3B5ZFpRd3ZRMFE6MA

Troubleshooting:

  • Check the spreadsheet or form in a browser where you aren't logged in to Google;
  • URLs starting http://spreadsheets.google.com are no longer supported (31 August 2011);

Limitations:

  • Wide spreadsheets will contain a scroll bar;
  • URLs containing 'gform' are not supported, eg. http://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/gform?key=VERY-LONG-KEY

Extra content

UPDATE: as of 8 December 2011, Google Docs presentations and documents can be embedded, as well as spreadsheets as forms.
