Using Video to allow students to recognise their achievements

  • Favourite
  • 915 views
http://www.vimeo.com/14501399

Cloud created by:

Derek Jones
1 December 2010

A really simple and effective way to summarise a body of work is to collate it visually. Even better if you can add a bit of music, a few keywords and make it all look like somthing has been achieved.

Using nothing more advanced than Picasa, some images of students' work and a bit of music, this video had a significant impact in an end of course tutorial. Students recognised their own contribution, saw it in a different context, and engaged in a genuine end of course reflection of their own personal journeys.

Dead simple to do (20 minutes), very effective (starts a great conversation about personal development), and a really nice way to end a course.
