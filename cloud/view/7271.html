[Adrian] My dream: Online Course about Creative Commons and OEP

  • Favourite
  • 816 views
  • 3 favourites

Cloud created by:

Adrian Stagg
14 January 2013

Context:

I currently run Professional Development sessions to support the goals of my Division.  The sessions are centred on Creative Commons, Open Educational Resources and Open Educational Practice.  Each session is curently designed to give an overview of the subject, but the main focus is having people from the different teams with the division work together on learnign activities and contribute to the discussion of eachs ession.  

There is also a call for these PD sessions to be offered to anyone in the university, not just our Division and possibly inlcusing these as part of our online academic induction.

The challenge:

The major challenge here is to take a format that works very well in-person and translate this experience (in some fashion) so that it is equally as rewarding when run online.  however, the online modules will be designed and developed to be run at any time of the year, and there is no guarantee that the session will have more than one particpant at a time (in fact this scenario is the basis of my 'productive paranoia'-based planning).

So... I'll be tackling the design of an Intoroduction to Creative Commons module that allows the particpant to contextualise the learning experience to their discipline and teaching needs.
