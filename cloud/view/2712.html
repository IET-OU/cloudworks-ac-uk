Online Information 2009

  • Favourite
  • 1264 views

Trade show at Olympia London with conference, not obviously about learning

Cloud created by:

Will Pollard
27 November 2009

First week of December for three days. Keynotes on the semantic web and social networks. The presentations near the stands are free, usually linked to product, but often find the issues ahead of the main conference.

Features an XML centre, a bit techie but crucial at this time.

More later, this is just here for people to add to.

Stands include OUP and Highwire.

It is not a learning event but the learning aspect keeps cropping up.

Next year same sort of format.
