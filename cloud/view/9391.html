The first Learning Design Cross Institutional Network took place on 7th of October 2016 at the Open University. The meeting was very well attended and members felt that there were many opportunities for this group to work closely together in the future.

The aims of the group/plans for further meetings were agreed:

    • interested in seeing (demos) tools and practices, and how they fit into institutional policies and strategies
    • Research/publications to be shared in the group
    • Information repository – Cloudworks to be used for now, this will be discussed further in the next meeting
    • Possible collaborative projects and funding bids
    • Barriers to overcome, for instance challenges in order to become more agile producing learning materials while also ensuring quality assurance.

Members showcased the following tools:

  • Workshop showcase: UCL Arena Blended Connect (ABC) curriculum design
  • Workshop showcase: OU Activity Planner 
  • Tool showcase: Greenwich Assessment Tool and research paper provided.

Next meeting (10.00 – 16.00) to take place in four months in London. Please complete the Doodle Poll to agree a meeting for February 2015 - http://doodle.com/poll/earmtvte4nihrv3i. Please note that indicating your availability does not register you automatically for the meeting. Once the data is confirmed please do confirm your attendance for catering purposes.

The presentations and other information can be found on http://cloudworks.ac.uk/cloud/view/9391.
