EdTech Europe 2015

22 April 2015

When: June 18th 2015

 

Where: Kings Place, 90 York Way, London N1 9AG

 

What: Europe’s leading education technology-focused conference platform www.edtecheurope.com. 

 

EdTech Europe connects the global EdTech community through an exclusive one day, multi-track summit that provides first-hand insight into the trends, opportunities and issues that surround the e-Learning industry. Offering extensive networking opportunities, the event brings together executive-level investors, innovators, and industry influencers from European and global education technology companies, while also aiming to connect leading foundations, higher-education institutions, policy makers, and thought leaders.

Now in its third year, EdTech Europe 2015 aims to host 500+ attendees and 60+ speakers, including a session powered by the US’ most energetic and innovative education sector event, SXSWedu, which will bridge the innovation divide between the European and North American continents.  Confirmed speakers are influencers from a wide range of education organizations and companies including TES Global, MIT Media Lab, the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, One Laptop per Child, Discovery Education and the Commonwealth of Learning.

The annual summit will showcase ideas related to industry, innovation, investors, and educators, through keynotes, panel discussion and workshops that address themes such as, Open Ecosystems, Dynamic Assessment, Predictive Learning Outcomes and Rich Media & Immersion.

EdTech Europe will also provide the stage for the culmination of the annual EdTech 20 competition; designed to foster and showcase notable innovation ideas within the start-up communities through pitches and awards.

For more information and to register, visit: www.edtecheurope.com.

To enter this year’s EdTech 20 ranking, visit: http://edtecheurope.com/edtech20/
