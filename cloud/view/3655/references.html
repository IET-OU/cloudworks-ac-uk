CALL FOR PAPERS

Journal of Computing ISSN 2076-0833

http://www.masaumnet.com/mjc.html

MASAUM Journal of Computing (MJC) is a peer-refereed journal for advanced, fully developed research in computer science, computer engineering, software engineering, system engineering, and information technology. MJC aims at publishing high quality research papers related to computer science, computer engineering, software engineering, system engineering, information technology and applications.

Researchers working in these areas worldwide are invited to contribute. We are interested in well-defined theoretical results and empirical studies that have potential impact on computer science, computer engineering, software engineering, system engineering, information technology and applications. MJC invites original, previously unpublished, research, survey and tutorial papers, plus case studies and short research notes, on both applied and theoretical aspects of computer science, computer engineering, software engineering, system engineering, information technology and
applications.

TOPIC OF INTEREST:


The areas of interests are but limited to:

Networks and Communications, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, System Engineering, Web Engineering,  Web Technologies, Databases, Data Warehouse, Data Mining, Soft Computing, Simulations, Modeling, High Performance Computing and Networking,  Distributed  Software Systems and Applications, Algorithms and Protocol, System, Security, Hardware and Software Performance Evaluation, Parallel Algorithms and Architectures, Software Design and Development, Software Tools and Techniques, Software Engineering Applications, Software Security, Optimization and Standardization, Intelligent Data Systems and Computing, Intelligent and Hybrid Control Systems, Adaptive and Robust Control, System Identification, Optimization, and Automation, Telecommunications Technology, Wireless Networks, Optical Communication Systems, Signal Analysis and Processing, Detection and Estimation, Audio and Video, Filters, Algorithms and Techniques.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:


Kindly send your camera ready version in Doc as well as in Pdf formats to masaum.jc@gmail.com before or on 30th May, 2010.

Kindly follow the instructions at:

 http://www.masaumnet.com/instructionsforauthors.html.

CONTACT US:
