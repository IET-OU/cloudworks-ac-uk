What didn’t you like or could be improved?

  • contact list so can reconnect with individuals
  • more consideration for participation on the periphery
  • ustream (or other method) / archive presentations
  • roles - like a "job jar" of ways to contribute to (expand on what we had: convenors, live bloggers, link posters, photographers, etc.)
  • Do we need a quick activity to get to know people?
  • publish institutional report notes (l think most had written notes)
