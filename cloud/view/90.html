Portfolios for professional development

  • Favourite
  • 1417 views

Keeping an ePortfolio gives students practice in planning and evidencing their professional development, and in creating skills-based CVs.

Cloud created by:

The Cloudworks Team
17 April 2008

Nurses and allied health professionals increasingly are required to keep portfolios to provide evidence of professional development. In this course, they are introduced to electronic portfolio-keeping on the university system. This enables them to add graphics or other digital records to their learning journal, and to share their completed portfolio templates with other students, to find out about each other’s practice, and with tutors, to get comment and feedback. An assessment requires them to use their ePortfolio to create a CV, thus matching their evidence of skills to the needs of the workplace. [Anthea Willson (formerly Anthea Harris)]
