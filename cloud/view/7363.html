A point of focus - context ...

2. Start to contextualise your project (Short route 45+ mins). Look at your week 1 project description (if you don't have a project choose one you like from the week 1 dreambazaar or create one now). Think of a story about learning related to the project: Who and what is involved? Why? When? Where? How? What happens? (Extended instructions)

1) Individually or in groups develop an initial scenario. If you want use this template and/or completed example. For an introduction to scenarios, see scenario-based learning design (Extended instructions).

2) Add your scenario/s to the scenarios cloudscape and/or display elsewhere.

3) Request feedback on your scenario & comment on others' scenarios.
