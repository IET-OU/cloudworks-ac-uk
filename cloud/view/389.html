Cloudscapes in Cloudworks

Cloud created by:

Gráinne Conole
21 November 2008

The cloudworks site has been significantly revised on 21st Novemeber. We are interested in getting feedback on the new site. A key aspect of the site is the inclusion of 'cloudscapes'. These enable users to aggregate content and discussion around a particular idea or issue. Users of cloudworks can follow different cloudscapes and clouds can be added to multiple cloudscapes, for each cloud it is possible to have a discussion around that cloud within the cloudscape. We would welcome your thoughts on this concept and on the new site.
