IET TCM Event: What I learnt from 'Flickr Photo a Day'

3 April 2012

Location: Jennie Lee Building Meeting Room 1 Ground Floor

Cloud created by:

Lynda Davies
29 February 2012

Abstract: Using services such as Flickr and Blipfoto, a practice has been established of taking, and sharing, a photo a day, over the period of a year. In this talk Martin and Juliette will talk about their experience of doing this, what they learnt and the implications for education.

Presenter: Professor Martin Weller

Location: Jennie Lee Building Meeting Room 1 Ground Floor
