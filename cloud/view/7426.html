"Think about what you aim to achieve this week in the MOOC. Post an item to your learning journal, listing your objectives, and noting how you will know that you achieved them."

  1. What were my objectives for this week?
    (1) Get an understanding of "Learning Design" in the context of my proposed project and create agenda for the following weeks by Wednesday.

    (2) "Follow the instructions" how to set myself up for this course properly
  2. How will I know when I have achieved them?
    (1) When I will have made a contribution to either the Mindmap and/or to the google groups discussion
    (2) When I finally figured out and made myself an infographic "what goes where and how are these things organized & interconnected
