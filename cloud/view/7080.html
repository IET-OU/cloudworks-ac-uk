Sue Whale: A Communications course for post grad business students

Situation/ context Developing a post grad course for business students on communication. Participants are professionals, mostly working in business already, aged 35+, studying part time, geographically disperse The course would be fully online via the institutional LMS Change I would like to see: Development of a sense of community within the course, through collaborative work or discussion How to bring about the change: Use constructivist approach, build activities that require teamwork???
