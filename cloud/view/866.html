Pedrotti SESSION NOTES:  NROC technology user groups

Key issues many members face:

 

  • Faculty adoption of NROC materials after license in place
  • Development of strategic implementation plan for OER materials
  • Formatting courses within various LMS’s
  • Availability of more granular content (“breaking open” learning objects is often necessary)

 

 

Suggestions from group for improvement/support:

 

  • Make materials more “editable” and encourage faculty to edit and or author materials in order to create a better sense of ownership
  • Connect NROC adoption to key organizational drivers at the strategic level as well as personal (get backing of vocal/influential faculty early on)
  • NROC community – Create self-help networking groups within online community specific to LMS and/or other third-party technology such as LOR
  • NROC community – create member directory that can include space for detailed institution profiles (projects, successes, challenges, needs)
  • Would like to have the ability to do subject-specific searches rather than just repository-wide federated searches
  • More granular learning objects
  • NROC community – place to share-back modified text to accompany media
  • NROC community – more member-lead webinars to address/discuss specific implementation issues
  • “beef up” player that works well with all LMS’s for easy content development
  • NROC community – (marketing) incentives for sharing-back content into the community
  • SCORM API in courses going forward
  • Support for open textbooks
  • NROC community – support for assessment-swap; formative assessments
  • Many requests for content “ready-made” for various LMS’s (Angel, Bb, Moodle, D2L, eCollege mentioned specifically)
