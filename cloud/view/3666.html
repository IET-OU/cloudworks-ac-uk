Writing a chunky learning outcome for Level 3

Target students group: English may not be students' first language (variety of students)

  • Different ways of delivering educational material
  • Different ways of engagement
  • Same level of outcomes for everyone
  • Knowledge: Locate: resources
  • Comprehension: search, explain and compare information relevant to the learning object, task and activity
  • relate information verbally and in written form on a blog or wiki
  • identify bullet points (key concepts)
  • writing abstracts

Extra content

Another approach to learning design is the macro- and micro-scripts in computer supported collaborative learning. They fit in the learning design, the macro script to describe the organisation convergence of activities and the micro on a cognitive level> http://www.springerlink.com/content/9q14234gx52x1784/
