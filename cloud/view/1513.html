A brief introduction to learning design

http://www.open.ac.uk/blogs/OULDI/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/Learn-about-learning-design_v7.doc

Cloud created by:

Simon Cross
27 April 2009

This five page document presents an introduction to learning design. It was written as a staff briefing paper in January 2009. There are sections asking what it is, who's doing it, how does it work, why is it significant, what are the drawbacks and links including to what the OU is doing.  The question of what a briefing/introduction like this should look like or how it could be developed may be topics for discussion below?
