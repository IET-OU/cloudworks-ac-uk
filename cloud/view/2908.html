  • Engaging staff and effecting cultural change and innovation in managing and developing people and institutional-staff relationships.

Chair: Chris Cobb, Roehampton University
Contributors: David MacLeod, Chair of the MacLeod Review on Employee Engagement; Sheila Gupta MBE, Edinburgh University

Extra content

Sheila Gupta (Edinburgh University) 1

How can HR add strategic value?

  • Link HR practices to business strategy & managing change
  • Foster cultures of creativity and innovation

High level management information:

Understand the business and the external environment

To create a culture of creativity & innovation:

  • decide that innovation is desirable
  • remove obstacles to creativity (eg: approval processes)

How to get employee engagement:

senior leaders must communicate the innovation agenda

act as role models

create fora to share & discuss innovative ideas

ensure channels to communicate ideas upwards & across

time, space and resources

IT systems provide easy access to information that prompts innovative thinking
