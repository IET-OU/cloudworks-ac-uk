  9. Do you have any other comments you would like to make?

Thank-you for taking the time to respond. This feedback will help us improve the module.

How long did you take to complete the Activity?

Difficult to say because I didn’t time it and I kept coming back to it on different days and having to re-read things.  I would estimate at least an hour and a half.

Were the instructions for the Activity clear? If they weren't how could they be improved?

What to read and think about was clear, but I was initially confused about who the course is for, as I feel in OU modules the module team have done the designing and I’m doing the delivering, so is this really for those who are right at the beginning of the process and not for me?  However, there is plenty of scope for me to modify and adapt my presentation of the module so it does seem relevant to understand design, and I like innovating and trying new things.

Was the content of the Activity informative? If not what would you have liked to see?

I found the content very informative and stimulating.  I would have liked clear reassurance that the course is for me.  Also whether the focus is on blended courses or pure online courses.

What did you like about the Activity?

I found it really interesting, and it ties in nicely with the OU masters in online education course H800 (Technology-enhanced learning: Practices and Debates) that I am currently doing.  I am getting to understand more about the opportunities provided by e-learning and am excited about this.

What did you dislike about the Activity?

Like Genie, having to fiddle around to print the material (and I realise this is not what we are ‘supposed’ to do, but I wanted to annotate it by hand!).  Having to log on to the public Cloudworks instead of having a private area.

Did you post any comments to the Activity Cloud? If you did not post any comments can you say why you didn't?

I did, and I also read the comments of others.  I would have liked to respond to some of the points, but it does not seem to invite this, as I cannot see how to make a comment on an individual message (perhaps I am missing something).  I like discussion more than a long list of individual answers.
