Keynote: Conole - Webinar on social inclusion, Web 2.0 and language education

  • Favourite
  • 1523 views
  • 1 favourite
Deadline: 27 April 2010

Presentation at Turku University

http://show.zoho.com/public/grainneconole/conole-turku-final-ppt

Cloud created by:

Gráinne Conole
24 April 2010

In this presentation I will explore the changing landscape of Web 2.0 practices. I will look at the shift from Web 1.0 to Web 2.0 and more user-centered, participatory Web practices. I draw on a recent review of Web 2.0 practices in HIgher Education and present a typology of Web 2.0 tool types. I will argue that there are also both advantages and disadvantages to these tools and will illustrate this point by exploring how Twitter and SecondLife are being used in an educational context. I then consider the implications for education, focusing on four themes:

  • Digital literacy skills
  • The importance of context
  • Institutional barriers
  • Digital identity

I conclude by putting forward a series of recomendations at three levels: teachers, institutions and national.
