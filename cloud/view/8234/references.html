http://cloudworks.ac.uk/cloud/view/6227. Lots of people linking from Clouds to twitter and talking about using Twitter in teaching, what personality they should have on Twitter etc.

Good reasons to use Twitter for study and teaching

 You get access to different points of view - follow people in your field and see what they are talking about, view their blogs etc and then see you they are following etc and connect with motre people.  

The people you follow will point you to great resources you might not find otherwise eg blogs, news, white papers, legislation, launches, conferences etc.

You can consult your followers (and others in the Twitter community) - ask questions, test ideas etc

Twitter is really like an updating search engine - search for key terms

Twitter helps you build study and professional networks  eg #highered (or higher education) or start one eg #H800.
