Katy Lilley: Representation Review

  • Favourite
  • 440 views

Cloud created by:

Katy Lilley
1 April 2015

 I decided to review the 4 Ts model and the e-Design Template. 

Readability –

I found the 4T's has an easy to understand linear flow. The diagram breaks up the content into more managable blocks of content with clear expectations & the timing from each T. It's adaptiblilty draws simplarities to Agile project Management.

The e-Design Template allows your to quickly identify if a lesson is focused more heavily one form of activity for example in this activity there is a large block based on guided Exploration. This style reminded me more of GANT charts and project management which made it quickly identifible for me.

On the review of both I prefer the layout of the 4T's as I feel the visuals are clearer and it encourages a variety of technologies to be used in the planning.

Expressiveness – The diagrams are well thought out and would allow people with and without previous subject matter to be able to identify what they would need to do.

Utility – The e-Design template looks at each activity as a whole and looks at the type of partcipation expected from the learner. Where as 4T's provides a more detailed look at what is required/expected of each resource i.e. the teacher & learner.

If I was designed a classroom training session I would most likely use the 4T's. However if I was looking to create a form of talent development plan that was based on both online and classroom training I would lean towards using the e-Design template so it would be possible to reflect on the balance of interactions.
