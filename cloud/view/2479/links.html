Personal Learning Environments & Personal Learning Networks

  • Favourite

Resources from a symposium and from research papers and pilots

Cloud created by:

Antonella Esposito
11 October 2009

This space is used to aggregate resources related to institutional, teaching and learning strategies focusing on the construction of PLEs and PLNs.

A symposium took place from October 13th till October 16th 2009 on Personal Learning Environments (PLEs) and Personal Learning Networks (PLNs). The interest in Personal Learning Environments has grown with the emergence of Web2.0 technologies. Learning technologists can see how PLEs can help learners to organize their own personal learning, rather than that formal education institutions control the technologies that are being used and the way in which they are being used. Speakers will include developers and researchers of PLEs: Graham Attwell, Stephen Downes, Josie Fraser, Lucy Gray, Mark van Harmlen, Rita Kop, Sharon Peters, George Siemens, John Thole, Sue Waters, Steve Wheeler, Scott Wilson.

All events were hosted in Elluminate and recorded for archives.

The event was being organized by George Siemens and Stephen Downes within the CCK09's activities.
