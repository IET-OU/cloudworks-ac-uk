Tell us how we should improve Cloudworks!

Cloud created by:

Juliette Culver
26 February 2009

If you encounter any problems with Cloudworks, post them here and I'll look into them. I'd also love to hear any idea for how to improve Cloudworks. 

  • We have a list of known issues and what we are working on.
  • You can also contact me directly at cloudworks@open.ac.uk 
  • Also would appreciate your feedback generally: cloudworks evaluation survey

 

Special note for Monterey 2009: I'm in the UK so the time difference may mean that it'll take me a little bit of time to get back to you, but I'll check this cloud and the e-mail address each day. This also means that if I have to get back to you to ask questions, it will slow down fixing any bugs by a large amount, so if it's urgent please give me as much information as possible.
