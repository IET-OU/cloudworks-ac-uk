NROC Network Meeting 2009 - Logistics

MITE Team
13 February 2009

THE NROC Network 2009 Member Meeting is open to representatives of all current NROC Network Members. The meeting begins Monday, March 2, 2009 at 9:00am and ends Tuesday, March 3, 2009 at 2:00pm. Attendees are also invited to stay for the opening activities of the Open Education Resources annual meeting Tuesday, March 3, 3:00pm – 6:00pm. There will be a project showcase and panel discussion  of OER projects from around the world that have been funded by the Hewlett Foundation (A special Advisors Dinner meeting will be held Sunday, March 1, 2009 at 6:00pm. This event is for NROC Advisors only.)

The NROC Network meeting is designed to be a highly interactive meeting, in which to focus is on sharing experiences and ideas and tackling common challenges among members. It will be made up of general and breakout sessions run by members and MITE staff. We will be using on online network before, during and after the meeting to capture highlights, images and short video clips of the event.

Location
The Beach Resort Monterey
Address:   2600 Sand Dunes Drive, Monterey, CA 93940 US
Phone: 831.394.3321 or 800.242.8627
Fax: 831.393.1912

How to book a room
Click on the Hotel's website link to book a hotel room, or call the hotel to make your reservation--just be sure to mention the meeting code. Group Name: MONTEREYTECH

Air Travel
Use your personal favorite travel agency or website to make your travel arrangements. The following airports are the closest to the conference (in order of proximity).

Monterey Peninsula Airport: MRY
For the Monterey Peninsula and the California Central Coast, regular flights are available to/from San Francisco, Los Angeles, Phoenix, Denver, or Las Vegas, where connecting flights can take you anywhere in the world. The four carriers that currently provide service from Monterey (American, United, US Airways, & Allegiant) are affiliated with airlines around the world. Travel time to the Beach Resort Monterey is about 10 minutes. Taxis are available outside of the airport.

Mineta San Jose International Airport: SJC
Travel time to Monterey from the San Jose airport: estimate 1 hour, 22 minutes, 76 miles.

San Francisco International Airport: SFO
Travel time to Monterey from the San Francisco Airport: estimate 1 hour, 47 minutes, 107 miles.


Optional shuttle from SFO to Monterey: You can arrange for a shuttle from San Francisco airport to the hotel through Monterey Airbus http://www.montereyairbus.com/.  For an additional $10 they will drop you at the meeting hotel.

 

Oakland International Airport: OAK
Travel time to Monterey from the Oakland Airport: estimate 1 hour, 52 minutes, 106 miles
