Prototype OER Module

Ann Davis
17 February 2013

This is part of a new hire curriculum for employees.  At this time, the students will have already had 2 weeks of formal classroom training and they will be back in the office getting practical experience for several days before returning to the classroom for additional training. This will be their "homework" during those office days.

Purpose: The purpose of this exercise is to use the knowledge gained while in the classroom, as well a knowledge within your own office to locate information or provide a solution to 10 common (and 2 uncommon) situations you might encounter on the job.

Directions:  Read each scenario, using all available resources (internal web sites, office mates, classmates, supervisors), locate or solve the items.  You will need to make a detailed note of the answers and then post to the class wiki. You do not need to solve all the items in order, however, please note that some have specific dates as to when the information is to be posted on the wiki.

Scenarios:  The list of information or scenarios will be given.

Wiki: Provide the wiki where they are to post their answers.

Post Discussion:  Once the students return to the classroom, they will go over as the class all of the items to see how each person got along.

Optional:  Different sets of scenarios could be given to the students so that more items can be covered.  Also, instead of just reading the answers on the wiki, may have the students discuss or show the others in the class how the found the information.
